What do you know about the human body?

 

In this unit, we're going to examine the science behind one of the human body's most critical structures for survival: organs. We'll also explore instances where our scientific understanding of organs and organ systems can make huge impacts on society. 

We use the word organ to describe a body part which carries out special functions within the human body. Much like the organelles within cells, each organ in our bodies has a unique purpose, many of which we will explore during this unit.

But before we go any further, let's take a moment to think of what we already know about this topic.
